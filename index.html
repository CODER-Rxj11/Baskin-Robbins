About Baskin Robbins !!!

Baskin-Robbins was founded in 1945 in Glendale, California
by Burton Baskin and Irvine Robbins, ice cream enthusiasts and brothers-in-law,
whose passion inspired what is now the world's largest chain of ice cream specialty shops.
